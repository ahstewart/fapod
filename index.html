Fake Astronomy Picture of the Day

Inspired by NASA's fantastic "Astronomy Picture of the Day" site, this is a silly excuse to explore ML applications for astronomical imaging.

Here I use a text-to-image model trained on the APOD archives to try and generate a fake image from the day's description.
